Privacy Statement

The one-man business Zubcevic.com IT consultancy & engineering (further abbreviated as Zubcevic.com) processes personal data. This privacy statement has been drawn up in response to the European privacy law GDPR that is known in the Netherlands as AVG General Data Protection Regulation. This privacy statement provides the most important questions and answers about how personal data is processed in this company. This privacy statement is valid from April 2018.

Contact details

You can contact this privacy statement with the owner via privacy @ zubcevic.com

From whom are personal data processed?

Zubcevic.com processes data from persons that the owner has direct contact with, such as:

  • Contacts of customers and / or clients
  • Contacts of partner organizations and / or business partners
  • Persons with whom in the past cooperated
No cookies are used on the websites of Zubcevic.com.

What is personal data processed for?

Personal data are processed for:

  • Maintaining business contacts
  • To meet legal obligations

How is personal data dealt with?

Personal data are only processed by the owner. Personal data are not shared with other parties. Personal data is stored safely and no longer than necessary with due observance of any legal storage periods.

Who is responsible for the processing of personal data?

The owner of Zubcevic.com is responsible for the processing of personal data.

Can I see which personal data is processed by me?

Anyone can ask if there are any personal data of them at Zubcevic.com. If so, an overview of the personal data will be sent. An application goes via an email to privacy@zubcevic.com If the request comes from an unknown email address, contact will be made on the basis of the known contact details belonging to the requested person. For people who are not known and who come from unknown e-mail addresses, there is no answer. An answer will follow within two working days, unless the amount of applications exceeds 1 per day.

Where can I go with a question or complaint?

You can contact the owner in the first instance via an email to privacy @ zubcevic.com or else to the Dutch Data Protection Authority https://autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl/
